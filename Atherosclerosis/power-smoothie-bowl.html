Probiotic Power Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of Greek yogurt (choose the one with live and active cultures)
  • 1/2 cup of fresh berries (blueberries, strawberries, or your favorite)
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 tablespoon of honey (optional, for sweetness)
  • 2 tablespoons of granola
  • 1 tablespoon of chia seeds
  • A handful of sliced almonds

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the Greek yogurt, ripe banana, and honey (if using). Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour the yogurt mixture into a bowl.
  3. Top it with fresh berries, granola, chia seeds, and sliced almonds.
  4. Finally, add a spoonful of sauerkraut or kimchi on the side or even on top if you're feeling adventurous.

Enjoy your probiotic power smoothie bowl! It's not only delicious but also great for your gut health.

This bowl is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a nutritious way to boost your gut health with probiotics. You can customize it with your favorite toppings and make it your own. Enjoy!
