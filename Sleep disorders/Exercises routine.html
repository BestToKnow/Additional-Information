Myofunctional Therapy Routine

Morning Routine (Approx. 10 minutes)

1. Tongue Retraction (2 minutes)

Sit or stand comfortably. Extend your tongue as far as possible. Slowly retract your tongue back into your mouth. Repeat this motion for 1-2 minutes.

2. Jaw Opening and Closing (2 minutes)

Keep your mouth relaxed. Gently open your jaw as wide as comfortable. Slowly close your jaw. Repeat this motion for 1-2 minutes.

Midday Routine (Approx. 10 minutes)

3. Buteyko Breathing Method with Trainer (5 minutes)

Use the Buteyko Breathing Method Trainer. Focus on controlled and shallow breathing. Inhale gently through your nose for a count of 2. Exhale slowly and completely through your nose for a count of 4. Repeat this cycle for 5 minutes.

Evening Routine (Approx. 30 minutes to 1 hour)

4. RespiBelt Respiratory Muscle Training (20-30 minutes)

Wear the RespiBelt during your evening exercises. Perform a combination of deep breathing exercises and resistance training. Follow these steps: Inhale deeply through your nose, expanding your diaphragm. Exhale slowly and completely through your nose, engaging your intercostal muscles. Repeat this deep breathing pattern for 10-15 minutes. Then, engage in resistance training by inhaling against the resistance provided by the RespiBelt for 5-10 minutes.

Before Bed Routine (Approx. 10 minutes)

5. Gentle Jaw Stretching (2 minutes)

Stand or sit comfortably. Gently open your mouth and stretch your jaw downward. Hold the stretch for 10-15 seconds. Repeat this stretch 3-5 times.

6. Relaxation Techniques (3 minutes)

Practice relaxation techniques such as deep breathing or mindfulness for 3 minutes to calm your mind before sleep.

Nighttime Routine (For Snoring, Bruxism, and Sleep Apnea)

7. AveoTSD Usage (if prescribed) (Throughout the night)

Follow the usage instructions provided by your healthcare provider or dentist. AveoTSD is typically worn during sleep to reduce snoring and sleep apnea events.

8. MyoBrace (if prescribed) (Throughout the night)

Follow the usage instructions provided by your healthcare provider or dentist. MyoBrace is often used at night to improve oral muscle function.

Please note that the use of AveoTSD and MyoBrace should always be prescribed and monitored by a healthcare professional. Adjust the duration and intensity of exercises and device usage based on your comfort and progress, and consult with a professional for personalized guidance if needed.
